About TechSuperNova

Welcome to TechSuperNova, the official technical quiz club of NIELIT Ajmer. Our mission is to foster a culture of innovation and knowledge through engaging quizzes and challenges in engineering and programming.

History of NIELIT Ajmer

The NIELIT Ajmer campus was established to advance technical education and skill development in electronics and information technology. Over the years, it has become a hub for innovative minds and aspiring engineers.
